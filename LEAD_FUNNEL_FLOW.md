# Lead Funnel Flow

## Complete Journey

```
Lead responds to jobs email
         ↓
   Stage 1: Jobs List Sent
         ↓
Lead clicks assessment link
         ↓
   Lead completes candidate assessment (13 questions)
         ↓
   Stage 2: Assessment Completed
         ↓
   ✉️ Auto-send: Skill Assessment Offer Email
         ↓
Lead clicks skill assessment link
         ↓
   Lead completes skill assessment (12 hard questions + 2 text responses)
         ↓
   Stage 3: Skill Assessment Completed
         ↓
   ✉️ Auto-send: Soft Pitch/Assessment Review Email
         ↓
   Stage 4: Soft Pitch Sent
         ↓
Lead responds positively
         ↓
   ✉️ Send: Training/Certification Offer Email
         ↓
   Stage 5: Training Offered
         ↓
Lead completes payment
         ↓
   Stage 6: Paid
         ↓
Lead completes certification
```

## Assessment Differences

### Stage 2: Candidate Assessment (Easy)
- 13 multiple choice questions
- Soft questions about experience, availability, motivation
- Entry-level friendly
- Takes 5-7 minutes
- Purpose: Basic qualification and engagement

### Stage 3: Skill Assessment (Hard)
- 10 hard multiple choice scenario questions
- 2 text response questions (50+ and 75+ words)
- Tests real remote work skills:
  - Customer service scenarios
  - Problem-solving under pressure
  - Professional communication
  - Time management
  - Accountability and independence
- Takes 15-20 minutes
- Purpose: Serious qualification for remote work readiness

## Stage Numbers (Clean & Sequential)

1. Jobs List Sent
2. Assessment Completed
3. Skill Assessment Completed
4. Soft Pitch Sent
5. Training Offered
6. Paid
7. Follow Up
8. Dropped

No more decimals (3.5, 3.6) - everything is clean integers!
No more "OFFERED" stages - emails are sent automatically when assessments complete!
